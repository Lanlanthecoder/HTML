Honey Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

It's easy and uses pantry staples. Always a hit with adults and kids. Serve with basmati rice or quinoa and steamed or roasted vegetables.

Ingredients (4 Portions)

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • Half a cup of soy sauce
  • Half a cup of ketchup
  • One-third a cup of honey
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Preparation Steps

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
